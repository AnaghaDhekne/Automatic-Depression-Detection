We Are Here To HELP

According to WHO report, India is the most depressed country in the world. Depression and mental illness is still not talked about in the open. In many situations people who are depressed are totally of their disturbed mental condition. They are unable to identify the cause of constant unhappiness in them and eventually such individuals dall into a state of mind where they start having suicidal tendencies. The De Depressie aims to provide early and accurate detection of depression for those who are suffering from mental health problems.
