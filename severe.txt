
A nationally representative survey conducted by Harvard Medical School to identify depression in Americans stated that 30% of Americans suffer from severe depression.Sometimes, such kind of depression is something you cannot control which include your genes, chemicals in your brain, your environment or a major life change.The best practice of such level of depression is to prevent it from getting worse.The first thing to do is to consult a psychological counsellor on an urgent basis.Medications and psychotherapy are effective measures to overcome such depressive symptoms.There is always a solution to any problem and so there is also a solution for defeating depression.Follow every schedule suggested by your therapist, regular intake of medications, develop a sleeping pattern, eat healthily, reach out to your family and friends when times get hard.All these measures if followed thoroughly will make you realize that there is always a brighter side to everything. Cheers!
