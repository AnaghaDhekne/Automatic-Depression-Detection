*What is Depression?
Depression is more than just feeling sad or pessimistic. Depression drains your optimism and energy.
It can seem like there's no way out. But no matter how bad you feel, there's always hope.

*What is BDI?
The Beck Depression Inventory (BDI) is a 21 questions, self-report rating inventory that measures
characteristic attitudes and symptoms of depression.

*What is BDI Score?
It is the score obtained by solving the BDI(Beck Depression Inventory) questionnaire.
